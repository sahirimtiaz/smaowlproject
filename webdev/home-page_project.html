Why is he my favorite player?

Virat Kohli is my favorite player because he has a amazing talent in batting and in my opinion he is the best bat's man that I know of. But Virat Kohli is arguably better than all the batsman. Kohli is cricket's latest superstar. Some of the numerous records to his name are: best ODI average ever (minimum 50 innings), second most centuries (41), fastest to 8000, 9000 and 10,000 runs, and the most 50-plus scores in T20I cricket (21).
